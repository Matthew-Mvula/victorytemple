Sunday Service

By Rev. Dr. Jim Botomani

17 March, 2024

God is able to do exceeding abundantly above all that we could ask for or imagine; but He does it, not according to His power in heaven, but according to His power that works in us. This means you have to get that power active and working all the time. In Acts 1, just before the ascension of the Master, He said to His disciples, “But ye shall receive power, after that the Holy Ghost is come upon you...” (Acts 1:8). To receive the power is the first thing; the next is to get it working.
